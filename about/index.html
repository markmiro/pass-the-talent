About us

Our Mission

Our passion is to curate and showcase talented performing, literary and fine artists from around the globe. To this end, we've chosen a safe vehicle via Periscope to offer artists the ultimate exposure. Periscope allows you to see life through someone else's eyes. Our eyes spot amazing talent and @PassTheTalent turns the spotlight onto these talented independent artists.

Beyond the showcase, PassTheTalent provides artists coaching, resources, an engaged community and ongoing support to develop artists.
