How it works

#PassTheTalent™ is Periscope’s #1 largest daily talent showcase, featuring genre-specific artists, live streaming, 6 nights a week at 6:30pm PST / 9:30pm EST.

What we venture to do on our platform, in partnership with PeriScout™ is curate, celebrate, and promote independent artists from around the globe, including vocalists, musicians, comedians, writers, and many other artistic broadcasters.

Each event is hosted by the Master of Ceremonies via the @PassTheTalent Periscope account, and moves on to feature a group of vetted artists using the “pass the cast” model. This format allows each artist to perform a 5-minute set, on their own personal account, before passing the scope on to the next artist, and so on and so forth, ending right back to the MC to wrap up the show.

Prior to each showcase, we send blasts over social media, giving devoted fans the opportunity to follow their favorite talent before the show begins. Which in turn, provides viewers who may have missed the live cast, the lineup for later playback, within the 24hr period..

If you would like more information, or are interested in joining an upcoming showcase, please DM @PassTheTalent on Twitter, contact #PassTheTalent™, or email info@passthetalent.com.
